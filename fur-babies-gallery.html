My Fur Babies

Samaun

My family was not into pets at first, especially my mother because she used to think that having pets like cats can bring bad odour to the house, and it will cause so much trouble raising up pets. However, a few years ago, there was a stray cat that came to my house. It was so skinny. It has succesfully made my mom feel sorry and fed it. At first, the cat was not allowed to come inside the house. As time goes by, my mom developed love for the cat and decided to adopt it. She brought the cat to the vet to get it vaccinated and neutered and finally it became the boss in the house and mom's favourite child. The cat is "Samaun".

We first took Samaun or Aun in 2017. From such a skinny kitten,to a fatty one, we really enjoy his company. However, in 2020, Aun has passed away. He did not have any illness. The cause of his death was probably because of age or obesity. We loved him dearly. It was really heartbreaking when he passed away. But as we always said to him, Aun will be waiting for us in Heaven.

Bella and Luna

Before Aun passed away, he was kidnapped. Alhamdulillah, we managed to get him back. However, Aun was no longer allowed to be an outdoor cat because my mom was afraid that he got kidnapped again. Because he looked so bored, my mother bought a kitten to be his friend, Bella. But being a big boss Aun was, he refused to befriend Bella. My mother end up getting another kitten to accompany Bella, Luna. We were unaware that at that time, there was a virus outbreak among cats called "Parvovirus". It is similar to Coronavirus for us and it is airborne. Bella was infected with Parvovirus before we were able to give her vaccination. It was not even 3 weeks since we first got her, but she was already sick. Not long after, Bella passed away. Luna also was infected not long after Bella was diagnosed. After 2 days of treatment, Luna followed Bella to the heaven. We did the best we could have done. We sent them to the vet for treatment but the outbreak was really bad. A lot of cats passed away because of this. At the end of the day, both of them passed away before Aun did, in November 2020. Now, they are reunited in Heaven.

Mocha and Mochi

My mom was so sad after losing Bella and Luna because she has grown fond towards them. Fast forward a few weeks later, a friend offered Mocha and Mochi for my mom. She was happy, but not given high hope that both of them would survive before vaccination. We took a really good care of them, and when they are eligable for vaccination, my mom immediately take them to the vet. Praises to Allah, they are doing healthy and fine up to today. Back then in March 2021, Mocha was diagnosed with Parvovirus like Bella and Luna did. This time, because she is vaccinated, the probability of her survival is rather high. After a week, she is free from the virus. Now that Aun, Bella and Luna are gone, they are the only ones that keeping my mother and I entertained everyday.
